## 📚 Individual Courses & Descriptions

### 1. [Course 1 - Design Fundamentals with AI](./Course%201%20-%20Design%20Fundamentals%20with%20AI/)
* **Description:** Explored fundamental principles of visual communication, focusing on layout design, grid systems, typography hierarchy, and color theory. Integrated cutting-edge generative AI tools such as Adobe Express and Adobe Firefly into the standard creative workflow to rapidly prototype concept sketches, generate mood boards, and accelerate early-stage visual asset creation.

### 2. [Course 2 - Generative AI Content Creation](./Course%202%20-%20Generative%20AI%20Content%20Creation/)
* **Description:** Mastered prompt engineering techniques and ethical AI design workflows using Adobe Firefly to build production-ready digital media assets. Developed hands-on experience creating high-impact visual content, fine-tuning generative prompts for stylistic consistency, and applying text-to-image and generative fill features to streamline overall marketing and design pipelines.

### 3. [Course 3 - Image Editing](./Course%203%20-%20Image%20Editing/)
* **Description:** Gained comprehensive, practical expertise in non-destructive image processing, photo retouching, and advanced digital compositing using Adobe Photoshop. Mastered layer management, vector and pixel masking, color grading, visual enhancements, and precision selections to produce professional, high-resolution imagery for print and digital channels.

### 4. [Course 4 - Graphic Design](./Course%204%20-%20Graphic%20Design/)
* **Description:** Focused on building scalable vector graphics, brand visual identities, and custom digital illustrations in Adobe Illustrator. Developed technical proficiency using the pen tool, Pathfinder operations, typography controls, and color swatches to craft high-quality logos, iconography, vector patterns, and comprehensive brand style guides.

### 5. [Course 5 - Document Design](./Course%205%20-%20Document%20Design/)
* **Description:** Learned multi-page publication design, editorial visual hierarchy, and document layout optimization for digital and print formats. Applied branding consistency, typography rules, grid alignment, and accessibility standards to produce professional marketing collaterals, digital brochures, multi-page reports, and interactive PDFs.
